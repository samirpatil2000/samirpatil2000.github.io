Getting Started

Up in 60 seconds.

1
Download Buffer.dmg using the button at the top or bottom of this page.
2
Open the DMG and drag Buffer.app into your Applications folder.
3
Launch Buffer — it appears in your menu bar. Grant Accessibility permissions when prompted.
4
Start copying as usual. Press ⇧⌘V anywhere to pull up your clipboard history.
⚠️ Note: Buffer is not notarized with an Apple Developer ID. On first launch, right-click the app → Open → click Open in the security dialog. This is a one-time step.
